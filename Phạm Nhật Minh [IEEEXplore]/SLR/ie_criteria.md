# IE Criteria - Large Language Models for Software Testing and Behavior-Driven Development
**Thành viên:** Phạm Nhật Minh
**RQ:** "How effectively can Large Language Models automate test case generation and Behavior-Driven Development in software engineering?"
**PICO:** P=Software Engineering (Software Testing, Behavior-Driven Development, Requirements Engineering) | I=Large Language Models (LLMs) and Generative AI | C=Traditional manual testing or rule-based test automation | O=Automated test cases, code coverage enhancement, executable specifications, and BDD formulation efficiency

## Inclusion Criteria (IC)
| Mã | Tiêu chí |
|----|---------|
| **IC-L** | Written in English |
| **IC-Y** | Published from 2023 to present – Reason: Modern Generative AI and Large Language Models (LLMs) effectively emerged and stabilized for software engineering applications in 2023. |
| **IC-T** | Published in a peer-reviewed conference or journal – not a blog, undergraduate thesis, or technical report |
| **IC-P** | Focuses on the task: Software Engineering (Software Testing, Behavior-Driven Development, Requirements Engineering) |
| **IC-I** | Utilizes the technique: Large Language Models (LLMs) and Generative AI |
| **IC-E** | Contains at least 1 empirical result or metric in a Table or Figure of the original paper |

## Exclusion Criteria (EC) – loại nếu BẤT KỲ điều kiện nào đúng
| Mã | Tiêu chí |
|----|---------|
| **EC-D** | Overlaps or is a duplicate of an existing paper in the selection list |
| **EC-A** | Full-text is unavailable or inaccessible |
| **EC-S** | Less than 4 pages (extended abstract, poster, short paper) |
| **EC-N** | Lacks empirical validation or experimentation (position paper, vision paper, tutorial) |
| **EC-O** | Out of topic: Autonomous Driving Systems, Domain-specific Chatbots (Tax support, Cooking), or Educational Essay Grading |
